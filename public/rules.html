Pick Time Game Time

Pick Time: Starts at 12:00 AM Tuesday EST and ends at 7:00 PM Thursday EST.

Game Time: Starts at 7:00 PM Thursday EST and ends at 12:00 AM Tuesday EST.

POINT SYSTEM

  • Push: +0.5 pts
  • Favorited moneyline win: +1 pt (points change to +0.5 pts if ML is -250 or higher)
  • Spread win: +1.5 pts
  • Underdog moneyline win: +2 pts (points change to +2.5 pts if ML is +250 or higher and +4 pts if ML is +400 and higher)
  • No penalty for normal pick misses

NO SAME PICKS

Specific picks are placed on a one week hold.

Ex: Picks can only be used bi-weekly, Chiefs ML week 4 can't be used again until week 6

BONUS PICKS

Immortal Lock: An optional 7th pick per week that can win you +1 pt or lose you -2 pts. (Spread, moneyline, underdog, favorite will all be counted as 1 pt if you chose to play this option)
