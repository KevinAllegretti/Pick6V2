Pick 6 Rule Book

RULES: “NO SAME BET”

Picks must be submitted by 7pm every Thursday.

Specific bets are placed on a one week hold. Ex: If Chiefs ML is picked, it can only be used bi-weekly, Chiefs ML week 8 can't be used again until week 10

POINT SYSTEM:

  • Push: +0.5 pts
  • Favorited moneyline win: +1 pt (points change to +0.5 pts if ML is -250 or higher)
  • Spread win: +1.5 pts
  • Underdog moneyline win: +2 pts (points change to +2.5 pts if ML is +250 or higher and +4 pts if ML is +400 and higher)
  • No penalty for normal pick misses

BONUS BETS:

Immortal Lock: An optional 7th bet per week that can win you +1 pt or lose you -2 pts. (Spread, moneyline, underdog, favorite will all be counted as 1 pt if you chose to play this option)
