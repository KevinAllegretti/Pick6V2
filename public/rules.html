Welcome to the Pick 6 Pool

OVERVIEW:

  • $10 Buy In.
  • Odds finalized upon commissioner release, no line changes.

RULES: “NO SAME BET”

Picks must be submitted by 7pm every Thursday.

Specific bets are placed on a one week hold. Ex: Chiefs ML/Spread can only be used once bi-weekly, Use Chiefs ML/Spread week 8 can't be used again until week 10

POINT SYSTEM:

  • +0.5pts for every spread push
  • +1pt for every favorited moneyline win (+0.5pts if ML is -250 or higher/ +1.5pts on top of the +2 if ML is +250 or higher)
  • +2pts for every spread win
  • +2pts for every underdog moneyline win

BONUS BETS:

Immortal Lock: An optional 7th bet per week that can win you +1pt or lose you -2 pts. (Spread, moneyline, underdog, favorite will all be counted as 1pt if you chose to play this option)
