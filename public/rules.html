Welcome to the Pick 6 Rule Book

RULES: “NO SAME BET”

Picks must be submitted by 7pm every Thursday.

Specific bets are placed on a one week hold. Ex: Chiefs ML/Spread can only be used once bi-weekly, Use Chiefs ML/Spread week 8 can't be used again until week 10

POINT SYSTEM:

  • +0.5 pts for every spread push
  • +1 pt for every favorited moneyline win (points change to +0.5 pts if ML is -250 or higher)
  • +1.5 pts for every spread win
  • +2 pts for every underdog moneyline win

BONUS BETS:

Immortal Lock: An optional 7th bet per week that can win you +1 pt or lose you -2 pts. (Spread, moneyline, underdog, favorite will all be counted as 1 pt if you chose to play this option)
